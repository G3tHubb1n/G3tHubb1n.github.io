Maddy C. grew up with a large, music-loving family. She took piano lessons from kindergarten through her junior year of high school. She started taking singing lessons in eighth grade, then taught herself guitar and ukulele in high school. She loves sharing her music.

"Music is my life. It is at the center of everything I do. I find that I hear music everywhere; I always have. As my singing instructor always says, "Performance is not done for accolades, but to bring others happiness." Her lessons gave me a deeper understanding of my passion and gave me more satisfaction when I perform. My true self shines when I play for others. My main goals are to find inspiration through others and share my songs."
