About



Music is my life. It is at the center of everything I do. I find that I hear music everywhere; I always have. As my singing instructor always said, "Perforamnce is not done for accolades, but to bring others happiness." Her lessons gave me a deeper understanding of my passion and gave me more satisfaction when I perform. My true self shines when I play for others. My main goals are to find inspiration through others and hopefully inspire a little myself.
